Pasta

Description

Pasta is a versatile and beloved staple of Italian cuisine made from wheat flour and water, sometimes enriched with eggs. It comes in a wide variety of shapes and sizes, such as spaghetti, penne, and fettuccine, each suited to different sauces and dishes.

Whether served with rich tomato-based sauces, creamy Alfredo, or simple olive oil and herbs, pasta is a comforting and satisfying meal enjoyed worldwide for its adaptability and delicious flavors.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Cook pasta according to package instructions and drain.
  3. Prepare the sauce by sautéing garlic and onions, then add tomato sauce, cream, or pesto.
  4. Mix the cooked pasta with the sauce and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs before serving.
