Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made by filling soft or hard tortillas with a variety of savory ingredients. They are typically served with toppings like salsa, cheese, lettuce, and sour cream.

Tacos are highly customizable, with options ranging from seasoned meat to vegetarian fillings, making them a popular and versatile food enjoyed by many around the world. They are known for their vibrant flavors and ability to be enjoyed as a quick snack or a full meal.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Protein
  • Toppings
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Warm soft tortillas or hard taco shells.
  2. Cook and season protein of choice (beef, chicken, fish, or plant-based options).
  3. Prepare toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, shredded cheese, and salsa.
  4. Assemble the taco by adding the protein and toppings to the tortilla or shell.
  5. Drizzle with sour cream or guacamole for added flavor.
  6. Serve with a lime wedge for extra zest.
